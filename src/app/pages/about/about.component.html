About Live Aid Serbia

Continuing the legacy of Live Aid through music, art, and humanitarian action

Our Mission

Live Aid Serbia is a humanitarian initiative inspired by the legendary Live Aid concert of 1985. We believe in the power of music and art to create positive change in the world. Our platform connects compassionate donors with meaningful causes, channeling support to communities in need across Serbia and beyond.

Our History

In 1985, the world witnessed an unprecedented event when music legends came together to combat famine in Africa. Live Aid raised millions and showed how entertainment could drive humanitarian action. Today, Live Aid Serbia carries this torch forward, focusing on local and global challenges that affect our communities.

1985: The Original Live Aid

The concert that changed the world, raising $127 million for famine relief.

2024: Live Aid Serbia Founded

Established to continue the Live Aid legacy in the Balkans region.

Today: Growing Impact

Supporting diverse campaigns from education to disaster relief.
